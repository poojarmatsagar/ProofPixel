About ProofPixel

ProofPixel is cybersecurity platform designed to detect phishing and malicious URLs before they can cause harm.

The Problem We Solve

Phishing attacks are one of the most common and dangerous cyber threats. Attackers create fake websites that look real to steal passwords, banking details, and personal data.

Many users cannot easily distinguish between a legitimate and a malicious URL this is where ProofPixel helps.

Why This Matters

  • Financial fraud & identity theft
  • Data breaches & account compromise
  • Increasing cybercrime year by year
